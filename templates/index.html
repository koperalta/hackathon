How BiyaheIQ Works

Mission

Problem We Solve

Opportunity inequality grows when commuting is unreliable, crowded, expensive, or unsafe. BiyaheIQ turns everyday mobility signals into practical intelligence so students, workers, and underserved communities can reach schools, jobs, hospitals, and services more effectively.
